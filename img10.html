“Unfolding”
Acrylic⁣ on wood
2018⠀
A fan is at its most beautiful and most useful when fully open.⁣⠀

I started these fans in 2017 and finally finished them late last year. I created them entirely from scratch—designed the blade shape in Illustrator, had them laser cut out of wood, then glued the blades together. I used acrylic to paint the base gradients and black linework. I wanted these to feel very freeform and organic and was heavily inspired by #Suminagashi —scanning and incorporating some actual marbling samples that I had made just weeks earlier at @japansociety_nyc. Happy with how these turned out!
