One of my favorite parts of the #MiiRxTanamachi collaboration is getting to work with my friends at @saintfrankcoffee once again! They graciously (and enthusiastically!) agreed to partner with us by creating the special edition La Florida roast (12oz bag), with its sweet & bright fruit flavors out of Colombia.

As with the rest of the collection, for each item sold, $5 goes directly to @preemptivelove as they provide COVID-19 relief to vulnerable populations around the world.

To learn more and/or pre-order, visit miir.com/tanamachi
