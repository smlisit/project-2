MiiR x Tanamachi Drinkware

Cross-post from Instagram

Introducing my new collaboration with Seattle-based drinkware company, @MiiR. This collection aims to showcase pieces of functional beauty which speak to the resilience and strength our global community has shown while battling an unprecedented pandemic.

Inspired by the Japanese concept of Gaman (我慢), translated as “enduring the seemingly unbearable with patience and dignity,” we have created a suite of custom products which help support @preemptivelove, a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting those most vulnerable to the effects of COVID-19. As a long-time friend & supporter of Preemptive Love and their incredible work, this is something I’m truly excited about.

For every #MiiRxTanamachi Collection product sold, $5 goes directly to @preemptivelove and their work around the world. To learn more and/or pre-order, please visit miir.com/tanamachi.
